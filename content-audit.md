# ARABAI Content Audit

Last updated: 2026-05-11

## Goal

Every article should help a normal Arabic-speaking beginner do one real AI task, not only understand an idea.

## Current Pass

The site now has:

- Arabic as the default homepage.
- Free articles with no login requirement.
- Beginner articles with plain analogies and copyable prompts.
- Use AI articles with real task scenarios, step-by-step workflows, prompts, tool links, and final outputs.
- Gamma article with real Gamma operation screenshots from prompt to outline, deck, share, and export.
- Writing and planning articles with ChatGPT process screenshots and final usable outputs.
- Image article with a finished poster output and clear image-2 workflow.
- Video article with the beginner method: text-to-image, 9-grid storyboard, then stitched video output.
- Music article with a playable audio output.
- Arabic article pages now reuse the same screenshots, outputs, video, and audio as the English article pages.

## Promotion-Ready Standard

Each article should include:

- A beginner-friendly task goal.
- A real-world example.
- Step-by-step action list.
- A copyable prompt.
- A short explanation of why the prompt works.
- At least one final result, such as text, table, image, deck, audio, or video.
- Official links or beginner tutorial links.
- Safety or checking notes where money, personal data, or business use is involved.

## Strongest Articles

- `make-slides`: real Gamma screenshot chain and final deck output.
- `write-with-ai`: ChatGPT process screenshots and final WhatsApp-style message.
- `make-a-plan`: ChatGPT process screenshots and final checklist.
- `make-videos`: 9-grid storyboard and final MP4 output.
- `make-music`: playable background music output.
- `create-images`: final poster output with image-2-oriented workflow.

## Still Needs Real Tool Screenshot Expansion

These pages are usable, but should later receive full real-operation screenshot chains from the named tools:

- `create-images`: add image-2 screenshots for prompt input, generation result, text correction, and download.
- `edit-images`: add real image editing tool screenshots for upload, prompt, before/after, and export.
- `make-videos`: add screenshots from the actual image tool and video editor timeline, not only storyboard and final MP4.
- `make-music`: add screenshots from the music AI tool showing prompt, generated track, and download.
- `image-tools-advanced`: add real screenshots from the image tool used most often by ARABAI.
- `video-tools-advanced`: add real screenshots from Seedance / Runway / Kling / preferred video tool.
- `music-tools-advanced`: add real screenshots from Suno / Udio / preferred music tool.

## Arabic Review Needed

Arabic content is functional and readable, but before heavy promotion it should be reviewed by a native Arabic editor for:

- Saudi/Middle East tone.
- Natural wording for beginners.
- UI labels and calls to action.
- Whether English tool names are written consistently.

## Do Not Change

- Articles remain free.
- Login is not required to read content.
- Credits and recharge are positioned as recorded/planned wallet functionality, not fully open payment.
- The site should stay beginner-focused, not developer-focused.
